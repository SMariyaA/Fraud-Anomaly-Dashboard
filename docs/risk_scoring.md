# Risk Scoring System

## Overview
- **Scale:** 0-100
- **Method:** Weighted rule-based scoring
- **Output:** Risk band (Low/Medium/High) + Top 3 reasons

## Risk Signals & Weights (Total 100)

| # | Signal | Weight | Trigger Condition |
|---|--------|--------|-------------------|
| 1 | Multiple Payment Failures | 15 | ≥1 failed payment before success |
| 2 | Suspicious Email Domain | 15 | tempmail, 10minutemail, guerrillamail |
| 3 | High Discount Usage | 10 | &gt;30% discount applied |
| 4 | Repeat Coupon User | 10 | &gt;3 unique coupons used |
| 5 | Unusual Quantity | 10 | &gt;95th percentile quantity |
| 6 | High Value Order | 10 | &gt;90th percentile order value |
| 7 | COD Payment | 10 | Payment method = COD |
| 8 | Multi-order Session | 10 | &gt;1 order per session |
| 9 | Late Night Order | 5 | Order time 11 PM - 5 AM |
| 10 | New User Account | 5 | Account age ≤7 days |

## Risk Bands

| Band | Score Range | Action |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Low | 0-30 | Auto-process |
| Medium | 31-60 | Enhanced monitoring |
| High | 61-100 | Hold for manual review |

## Calculation Example

**Order with:**
- Payment failures (1): 15 points
- High discount: 10 points
- COD payment: 10 points
- Late night: 5 points

**Total: 40 → Medium Risk**

## Top 3 Drivers
Automatically generated by checking which signals triggered, ranked by weight.
